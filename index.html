What I Do Now

Business Edits

Business Edits is my main focus. I work closely with founders, operators and small teams building consumer products, platforms or services who want to make better decisions earlier.

The work is practical and collaborative. It often sits where things feel slightly stuck or fuzzy.

  • Strategy and decision making
  • Brand and narrative clarity
  • Product and commercial thinking
  • Structure, systems and priorities

I bring an operator’s perspective shaped by building and running a venture backed consumer brand, owning P and L, managing teams and raising capital. I use AI daily to accelerate research, stress test ideas and improve execution, but judgement always comes first.

“Tools change. Clear thinking still does the heavy lifting.”

Selected Work

DAME

I co founded DAME to address two problems hiding in plain sight: poor design in women’s health products and the environmental cost of period care.

DAME created the world’s first reusable tampon applicator and built a global consumer brand around women’s health and sustainability.

  • Launched via Kickstarter, exceeding the funding target by 600 percent
  • Global press and product awards including Dezeen
  • National retail listings with Waitrose, Sainsbury’s, Boots, Ocado, Selfridges, ASOS and Next
  • Reached 5 percent revenue share from Tampax within six months of launch in Waitrose
  • Customers in more than 50 countries
  • Ecommerce growth of 150 percent year on year
  • Raised over £2m from angels, corporate venture capital, family offices and crowdfunding, with Sky as an early investor
  • Early B Corp certification
  • Prevented more than 20 million disposable applicators from entering landfill

Alongside the commercial work, I built and led the team, set strategy, managed culture, and owned company wide finance and P and L. I also founded DAMEchangers, an innovation camp for 16 to 17 year old girls at Oxford University, and represented the brand through public speaking, media and UK Government taskforces.

How I Think

I am sceptical by default and optimistic by choice.

  • Framing the right problem before chasing solutions
  • Making complexity simpler without losing meaning
  • Designing systems that last longer than enthusiasm
  • Using evidence and numbers as allies, not threats

I am comfortable changing my mind when something better shows up. Experience has taught me that confidence comes from learning fast, not pretending to know everything.

“Most mistakes start as assumptions that were never questioned.”
